Artificial intelligence may be making us think and write more alike, according to a new study. Large language models are standardizing human expression and subtly influencing how we think. Researchers argue that AI developers should incorporate more real-world diversity into LLM training sets to preserve human cognitive diversity and improve chatbots' reasoning abilities. 

<Callout title="Key Takeaways" type="info">

* AI may be reducing humanity's collective wisdom and ability to adapt

* LLMs are standardizing human expression and thought

* Incorporating diverse training sets can improve chatbots' reasoning abilities

* Preserving cognitive diversity is crucial for creativity and problem-solving

</Callout>

## What Is Cognitive Diversity & Core Benefits

Cognitive diversity refers to the variety of different perspectives, ideas, and thought processes within a group or society. It is essential for creativity, problem-solving, and innovation. However, cognitive diversity is shrinking worldwide as billions of people use the same handful of AI chatbots for an increasing number of tasks.

### The Importance of Cognitive Diversity

Cognitive diversity is vital for groups and societies as it bolsters creativity and problem-solving. When people use chatbots to help them polish their writing, for example, the writing ends up losing its stylistic individuality, and people feel less creative ownership over what they produce.

## Technical Deep Dive

Large language models are trained to capture and reproduce statistical regularities in their training data. However, this can lead to a narrow and skewed slice of human experience being reflected in their outputs. 

### How LLMs Work

LLMs are trained on vast amounts of text data, which enables them to generate human-like language. However, this training data often overrepresents dominant languages and ideologies, resulting in LLM outputs that mirror a narrow and skewed slice of human experience.

### The Impact of LLMs on Human Thought

The concern is not just that LLMs shape how people write or speak, but that they subtly redefine what counts as credible speech, correct perspective, or even good reasoning. This can have significant implications for human thought and expression.

## Real-World Applications

Companies are using LLMs in various applications, such as chatbots, language translation, and content generation. However, the use of LLMs also raises concerns about the potential homogenization of human thought and expression.

## Industry Perspective

The developer community is divided on the issue of LLMs and their impact on human thought. Some argue that LLMs can improve human productivity and efficiency, while others believe that they pose a significant threat to human cognitive diversity.

## Pros, Cons & Trade-offs

| Pros | Cons |
| --- | --- |
| Improved productivity | Homogenization of human thought |
| Enhanced efficiency | Reduction of cognitive diversity |
| Increased accuracy | Dependence on technology |

## Step-by-Step Implementation

To incorporate diverse LLM training sets, developers can follow these steps:
1. Collect and annotate diverse text data
2. Train LLMs on this diverse data
3. Evaluate and fine-tune LLM performance
